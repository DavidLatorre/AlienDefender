Alien Defender X

Move your tank using the mouse on the left side of your screen and shoot with click, also, you can use the keyboard arrows to move and backspace key to shoot, your goal are destroy all alien spaceships

this game was designed by David Latorre, you can found the opensource (This mini-game are FOSS!!!) on this github repo
